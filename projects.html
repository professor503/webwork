Founded in 1992, ARCHITECH began as a small design studio in a refurbished warehouse in Chicago, established by visionary architect Naeem ur Rahman and structural engineer Clara Moreno. United by a passion for innovation and timeless design, their mission was clear:to challenge the boundaries of traditional architecture while honoring the rich legacy of classical form . In its early years, ARCHITECH focused on urban revitalization projects, breathing new life into aging industrial spaces. By the late 1990s, their reputation for sustainable design and creative problem-solving had earned them national acclaim. The company expanded internationally in the early 2000s, opening studios in Berlin, Dubai, and Singapore, and taking on a diverse portfolio of work — from high-rise commercial towers to award-winning cultural landmarks. ARCHITECH became synonymous with forward-thinking architecture, blending technology, art, and nature. Over the past three decades, ARCHITECH has grown into a multidisciplinary firm, with a team of architects, planners, interior designers, and urban strategists. The firm's signature style is marked by clean lines, innovative use of materials, and an unwavering commitment to sustainability.
Today, ARCHITECH continues to lead the future of architecture, staying true to its founding belief: “Great architecture is not only built — it is felt.”
