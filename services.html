---------------------Services provided--------------------

We typically provides a range of services related to
the design, planning, and oversight of building projects.
These services can vary depending on the firm’s size
and specialization, but generally include:



  • Architectural Design

    • Concept Design: Initial sketches and ideas to explore the project's vision
    • Schematic Design: More detailed drawings and planning of spaces and layout.
    • Design Development: Refinement of the chosen design with input from engineers and consultants.
    • Construction Documents: Full technical drawings and specifications used for permits and construction.



  • Feasibility Studies & Master Planning

    • Evaluation of a site or building to determine if a project is viable.

    • Includes zoning analysis, building code compliance, and budget estimation.
    • Large-scale planning of sites or developments (e.g., campuses, districts)
    • Addresses infrastructure, land use, and future growth.



  • Project Management & Construction Administration

    • Renderings, virtual reality, and animations to present the design.

    • Site visits and inspections.Ensuring construction follows design intent
    • Passive design strategies and energy modeling.
    • Renovating historic buildings while maintaining their character.



  • Urban Design & Landscape Architecture

    • Designing outdoor spaces and urban environments.

    • Streetscapes, plazas, parks, and public realm improvements.






  • Further Services

    • Historic Preservation & Adaptive Reuse

    • Sustainability & Green Building Consulting
    • 3D Visualization & Modeling
    • Permit & Code Compliance
